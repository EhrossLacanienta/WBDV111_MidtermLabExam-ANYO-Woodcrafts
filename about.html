About Us

Learn more about our story, mission, and values

Our Story

ANYO WOODCRAFT is an online retail business established in 2020 and based in Taytay, Rizal, Philippines. What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted source for personalized wooden gifts and laser-engraved products.

The company specializes in creating meaningful handcrafted gifts for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, graduations, and corporate events. Each piece tells a story and carries the warmth of natural wood combined with modern laser engraving technology.

We offer custom wooden souvenirs, corporate giveaways, and meaningful handcrafted gifts tailored to customer preferences. Whether you're looking for a thoughtful personal gift or bulk corporate orders, we bring your vision to life.

Our Mission

With a strong focus on quality craftsmanship and customization, ANYO WOODCRAFT provides affordable yet memorable gift solutions for students, professionals, families, event organizers, and corporate clients nationwide.

The brand primarily operates through Shopee and Facebook and aims to expand its presence by building a dedicated website to improve product showcasing and customer relationships.

Our goal is to make personalized wooden gifts accessible to everyone, ensuring that every special moment can be celebrated with a unique, handcrafted treasure that will be cherished for years to come.
